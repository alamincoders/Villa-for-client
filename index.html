Villas for Babies and Toddlers

If you're holidaying with little ones, you want to know that you're staying somewhere that takes their safety seriously. From gated pools to single storey properties with all the safety essentials taken care of, our baby and toddler friendly villas are the perfect way to recharge your batteries while discovering exciting new destinations as a family.

Baby and Toddler Friendly Villas

We appreciate travelling with a toddler in tow can feel more like a military operation, and less like a holiday. Which is why our Baby and Toddler villas have been handpicked to give your family a safe and relaxing time away together. Find out more

Marco

Vale de Parra, Algarve

*Part of the 'Baby & Toddler' villa range* Located in a quiet area of Vale de Parra this highly recommended villa is within a short walk of a few bars and restaurants while the sandy beaches of Gale and Castelo are within a 5 minute drive.

Son Marc I

Sa Pobla, Mallorca

*Part of the 'Baby & Toddler' villa range* Situated in a rural location and set amongst mature gardens Son Marc I offers peaceful accommodation surrounded by agricultural land, perfect for those looking to get away from it all!

Nuria

Cala en Bosch, Menorca

This charming, traditional style villa is conveniently positioned in the area of Cap D' Artrux, within the resort of Cala en Bosch, and a short walking distance of the resort amenities.

Guillermo

Pollensa, Mallorca

*Part of the 'Baby & Toddler' villa range* This attractive villa is set amongst mature gardens and conveniently situated within easy reach of Pollensa and Puerto Pollensa, both of which offer a variety of bars, cafes and restaurants.

Le Flamboyant

Binibeca, Menorca

*Part of the 'Baby & Toddler' villa range* Le Flamboyant is a wonderful property that offers privacy and comfort for large families or groups of friends. The beach is just a short distance away.

Crofton Springs

New Providence, Orlando - Florida

This fantastic newly built house is situated on the exclusive gated community of Providence resort and has been specially designed to suit families including wheelchair users.
